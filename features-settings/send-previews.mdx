# How To Easily Send Previews

![send-preview-1.png](/images/send-preview-1.png)

**Sending email previews couldn't be easily, located directly off the Tools Menu.**

 Simply enter your desired subject line and add the email addresses of up to five recipients to distribute a test version of your campaign. With the added convenience of storing the last 10 recipients you've engaged with, our system eliminates the need to manually re-enter email addresses for each test. This seamless process enables you to gather valuable feedback, verify email placement, and ensure your message resonates as intended before the final send-off.
