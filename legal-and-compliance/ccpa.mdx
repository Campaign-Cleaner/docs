**Effective Date:** 5/1/2023

At [Campaign Cleaner](https://campaigncleaner.com) LLC, we respect and value the privacy of our users. This CCPA Compliance Document outlines our practices concerning the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information of California residents in accordance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

## Information We Collect ##

Campaign Cleaner LLC collects information that identifies, relates to, describes, references, is capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked, directly or indirectly, with a particular consumer or device ("personal information"). The categories of personal information we collect include identifiers, commercial information, internet or other similar network activity, and professional or employment-related information.

## Personal Information We Do Not Collect ##

Our software is designed for the sole purpose of processing and analyzing data related to email campaigns. These email campaigns should not contain any personal information as defined by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). This includes, but is not limited to, personal identifiers, characteristics of protected classifications under California or federal law, commercial information, biometric information, internet or other electronic network activity information, geolocation data, sensory data, professional or employment-related information, non-public education information, or inferences drawn from other personal information.

As such, we do not intentionally collect, process, or store personal information through our software. Our users agree to these terms in our Terms of Use and are responsible for ensuring that the content of their email campaigns complies with this policy. In the event that we become aware that personal information has been processed through our software, we will take appropriate steps to remove this data in accordance with our data handling policies and applicable laws.

## Use of Personal Information ##

We may use the personal information we collect for business purposes, including to optimize your email campaigns, provide customer service, process transactions, and improve our services.

## Sharing Personal Information ##

Campaign Cleaner LLC may share your personal information with service providers or third parties for business purposes. When we disclose personal information for a business purpose, we enter a contract that describes the purpose and requires the recipient to keep that personal information confidential and not use it for any purpose except performing the contract.

## Your Rights and Choices ##

The CCPA provides California residents with specific rights regarding their personal information. These include the right to request that Campaign Cleaner disclose certain information to you about our collection and use of your personal information over the past 12 months, and the right to ask that we delete any of your personal information that we have collected from you and retained, subject to certain exceptions.

## How to Exercise Your Rights ##

To exercise your rights under the CCPA, please submit a verifiable consumer request using one of the contact methods listed here: https://campaigncleaner.com/contact.html

Campaign Cleaner LLC reserves the right to amend this CCPA Compliance Document at our discretion and at any time. When we make changes to this CCPA Compliance Document, we will post the updated notice here on our website and update the notice's effective date.
